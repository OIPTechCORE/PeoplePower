## üéØ **OVERVIEW & VISION**

### **üåü What Are People Power Movement Tokens?**

People Power Movement Tokens are **purpose-driven cryptocurrency tokens** that represent real-world social movements, community initiatives, and causes. Unlike meme tokens that are based on internet jokes and speculation, Movement Tokens create tangible social impact through:

- **Community Ownership**: Token holders own and govern movements
- **Real-World Impact**: Tokens fund actual community projects
- **Democratic Governance**: Holders vote on movement decisions
- **Educational Integration**: Learning and skill development
- **Cross-Border Engagement**: Global diaspora participation

### **üèÜ Why Movement Tokens Beat Meme Tokens**

| **Feature** | **Meme Tokens** | **People Power Movement Tokens** |
|-------------|----------------|----------------------------------|
| **Purpose** | Entertainment & speculation | Social impact & community building |
| **Value** | Market speculation only | Tangible community benefits |
| **Governance** | None | Democratic voting & decision making |
| **Longevity** | Short-term hype cycles | Sustainable community growth |
| **Impact** | None | Measurable social change |
| **Education** | None | Integrated learning systems |
